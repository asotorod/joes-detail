Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How does mobile detailing work?

+
It's simple! You book an appointment, and we come to your location ‚Äî whether it's your home, office, or anywhere else convenient for you. We bring all our own equipment, water, and supplies. All we need is access to your vehicle and we handle the rest. You can go about your day while we make your car shine!

What areas do you service?

+
We service the entire Pocono region including Marshalls Creek, Saylorsburg, East Stroudsburg, Stroudsburg, Tannersville, Mount Pocono, and surrounding areas. If you're unsure if we service your area, just give us a call and we'll let you know!

How long does a detail take?

+
It depends on the service and condition of your vehicle. An Express Wash typically takes 45 minutes to 1 hour. Interior details take 2-3 hours. Complete details can take 3-5 hours. Ceramic coatings require a full day. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept cash, all major credit cards, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment is due upon completion of service. For larger jobs like ceramic coatings, we may require a deposit to secure your appointment.

How often should I detail my vehicle?

+
For best results, we recommend an Express Wash every 2-4 weeks, an Interior Detail every 3-4 months, and a Complete Detail twice a year (spring and fall). Regular maintenance keeps your vehicle looking great and protects its value. We also offer maintenance packages for regular customers!

What if it rains on my appointment day?

+
We monitor the weather closely. If rain is expected, we'll contact you to reschedule at no charge. For covered locations like garages or carports, we can often still work in light rain. Your satisfaction and the quality of our work always comes first!
